Key Body Metrics We Analyze

How Our Body Analysis Works

Step 1: Initial Assessment

We begin with basic checkups like height, weight, lifestyle, and fitness goals.

Step 2: Digital Body Scan

Our advanced machine scans your body to calculate fat, muscle, water, and bone density.

Step 3: Detailed Fitness Report

You receive a complete results sheet along with expert recommendations to improve your body composition.

Why Body Analysis is Important

Set the Right Goals

Know exactly what to target—fat loss, muscle gain, or strength improvement.

Improve Overall Health

Track internal progress like metabolism, water levels, and visceral fat.

Measure Real Progress

Stop guessing — see actual changes with every scan and report.

Personalized Recommendations
