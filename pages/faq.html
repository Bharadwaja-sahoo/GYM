Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our gym services, membership, and training programs.

  1. Home
  2. Frequently Asked Questions

What are the gym’s membership plans and pricing options?

We offer monthly, quarterly, and yearly membership plans with flexible pricing based on your fitness goals. You can also choose add-ons like personal training, group classes, or nutrition guidance. Contact us or visit the gym for the full plan details.

Do I need previous workout experience to join?

Not at all! Our gym welcomes beginners, intermediate, and advanced fitness enthusiasts. Our trainers guide you through proper form, basic exercises, and a personalized routine to help you start safely and confidently.

Are personal trainers available?

Yes, certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one coaching. They help with weight loss, strength training, muscle gain, mobility, and overall fitness planning based on your goals.

What facilities and equipment are included?

Our gym includes cardio machines, strength machines, free weights, functional training zones, group workout areas, and locker rooms. Some memberships also include access to steam, showers, and special classes.

Can I freeze or cancel my membership?

Yes, memberships can be frozen for medical or travel reasons. Cancellation policies vary depending on your membership plan. Please check with our support team for specific terms and conditions.

Still have questions?

If you didn’t find the answers you’re looking for, our support team is always here to help. Feel free to contact us anytime for membership info, schedules, or fitness guidance.
